What is Montessori?

Montessori is an education philosophy and practice that fosters rigorous, self-motivated growth for children and adolescents in all areas of their development, with a goal of nurturing each child’s natural desire for knowledge, understanding, and respect.

For over a century, Montessori materials have been used to teach children with great success. Montessori materials are similar across the globe and are consistent in the way they are presented. They are displayed on open, easy-to-reach shelves in the classroom.

These materials are attractive and complete with no part missing. The children work with the materials in a particular order, from simple to complex, enabling them to gain mastery in a progressive manner. 

They are a match to the natural curiosities of a child and are designed to support a part of their development. As they respond to their natural curiosities and explore the materials they have been introduced to in the classroom, they develop a life-long love for learning. 

In a Montessori classroom, children do their work on mats  or on child-size tables. This helps the children to delineate their work spaces and stay organized. The children are also off​ered a variety of learning experiences ranging from individual learning, group learning and multi age learning.

Montessori Philosophy

Montessori offers children a hands-on learning experience which happens to be one of the best possible means of learning for early years. 

There is a lot of science that shows a strong connection between the hands and the brain. 
The brain gives instruction to the hands, which then give information to the brain, 
a continuous cycle in the development of the intellect

Christian Education

The goal of any school is to educate children about life, the world, and their purpose in it. 
Christian education seeks to teach children about the world God created, how our faith is woven into each area of learning, and how to find purpose and meaning through God’s Will for our life. 
We believe that only through an understanding of God, His Word, and His works, can we know truth and show His love to those around us.
