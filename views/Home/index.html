Who We Are

Holm Oak is a Christian Montessori school that nurtures and trains children in a happy, engaging and safe environment.

Our Aim

Our aim is to help the children to develop a love for learning and build strong academic and spiritual foundations that will empower them to achieve their full potential. 

“We must see the child from the point of the future man.”
- ​Dr. Maria Montessori, founder of the Montessori curriculum

Why Montessori

The Montessori methodology is one of the most intelligent and innovative methods of teaching children. 

It offers a methodology of learning that supports our goal of providing a strong academic foundation to the children.

learn more

Why Christian Education?

We integrate Christian principles and education into the children’s everyday learning because we envision a generation who are OAKS OF RIGHTEOUSNESS.
